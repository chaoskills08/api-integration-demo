A video game and food generator application.

Have you ever wondered what game you should play? Better yet, have you ever wondered what you should eat while playing said game? Well luckily for you, we solved those issues here at GamerFoods! All you need to do is click one of the five buttons below and our site will generate a game along with its description and also a food item along with its recipe!
